My Aim

Educating children is undoubtedly one of the most essential activities in the modern world, because today's children are tomorrow's future, and their development is critical for a brighter future. My goal is to help all nuero-diverse learners overcome obstacles and reduce their struggle during learning. I want to help children with Speech impairment, Visual perceptual/visual motor deficiency, dyslexia, intellectual impairments, learning disabilities, and other special needs feel mentally protected and empowered so that they can communicate and learn better.

How this website helps

Through this website's two main parts nuerodiverse learners can enhance their communication and understanding skills. it's two main parts are:

Enhancement of pronunciation:
A section of my website assists individuals with diverse learning capacities in improving their pronunciation by saying the phonetics sounds aloud and testing to determine if they are accurately pronouncing them.This website's assistance allows the customer to effortlessly conquer their difficulties.
Improving learning abilities
This part of my website helps neurodiverse learners to practice and improve their visual information processing capacity. This aspect of my website aids neurodiverse learners in strengthening their ability to interpret visual data as they use the objects in their environment in integration with the website to learn more effectively.

About the website

Enhancement of pronunciation: Here you can say out loud the sound or name of the letter which will result in the display of that letter's cursive capital and lower case handwriting image. This way the children using this app can both improve their pronounciation and handwriting as they can see the letter of the sound that they are pronouncing and correct themselves if wrong and also instantly view the written version of the word in their head.
Learning basic topics such as colors, shapes, number: Here they can learn colors, shapes etc in a very visual manner increasing their practice in visual information proccessing. This aspect of my website aids neurodiverse learners in strengthening their ability to interpret visual data as they use the objects in their environment in integration with the website to learn more effectively.They can show an object towards their webcam and see it's color, name, shape etc.
In addition In the list at the end of te website, link to apps that aid in visual information proccessing in simple topics such as numbers, animals etc are present, clicking on them will direct you to their respective screen. Apart from these apps, a website where the user can trace a cursive letter is present, this would definitely, greatly aid children who have trouble with handwriting.
